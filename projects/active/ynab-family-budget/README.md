## Core Objective

Use YNAB to create and maintain balanced family budget where income = budgeted, all spending tracked, and financial goals met.

## YNAB Four Rules

1. **Give Every Dollar a Job** - Assign all money to categories
2. **Embrace Your True Expenses** - Break annual costs into monthly
3. **Roll with the Punches** - Adjust categories flexibly
4. **Age Your Money** - Spend money earned 30+ days ago

## Weekly Routine

**Sunday Budget Review** (15-20 min)
- Reconcile all accounts
- Enter missing transactions
- Review past week spending
- Adjust categories if needed

**Daily** (2-5 min)
- Enter/approve transactions
- Categorize spending
- Check budget before purchases
