## YNAB Four Rules (Core Methodology)

### Rule 1: Give Every Dollar a Job
- Assign all available money to categories until nothing left
- No money sits unassigned
- Every dollar has a purpose before it's spent

### Rule 2: Embrace Your True Expenses
- Break large, infrequent expenses into monthly amounts
- Example: $1,200 annual insurance = $100/month category
- Prepare for predictable "unexpected" expenses

### Rule 3: Roll with the Punches
- When overspending happens, adjust other categories
- Budget is flexible, not restrictive
- Move money between categories as needed

### Rule 4: Age Your Money
- Goal: Spend money earned 30+ days ago
- Creates buffer between earning and spending
- Reduces financial stress and paycheck-to-paycheck cycle

## Common YNAB Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: Too many imported transactions to categorize
**Solution**: Spend 10 minutes daily rather than letting them pile up

### Challenge: Forgetting to enter cash transactions
**Solution**: Enter immediately using mobile app, or avoid cash

### Challenge: Budget feels restrictive
**Solution**: Remember Rule 3 - move money between categories freely

### Challenge: Overspending in certain categories
**Solution**: Either fund more or identify spending to reduce

### Challenge: Partner not engaged
**Solution**: Focus on shared goals, make it collaborative not controlling

### Challenge: Irregular income (varies month to month)
**Solution**: Budget conservatively, hold excess in "income for next month" category
