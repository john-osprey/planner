# Family Midwinter Training Project

A structured training program bringing Dad (Boston Marathon prep) and boys (ages 12 and 10, baseball offseason) together through shared fitness, dialogue, and quality time.

## Quick Links

- [Full Project Details](family-winter-training.md)

## Project Status

**Training Period**: December 2025 - March 2026 (16 weeks)
**Status**: Active - Just Started

## Core Goals

1. **Individual Excellence**: Dad trains for Boston Marathon, boys build baseball fitness
2. **Shared Experience**: Weekly family runs and bodyweight strength sessions
3. **Quality Dialogue**: Saturday morning training check-ins
4. **Habit Building**: Sustainable fitness routines for all
5. **Relationship Building**: Use training as bonding opportunity

## Weekly Routine

- **Sunday AM**: Family easy run (10-20 min for boys, Dad continues)
- **Tuesday/Thursday PM**: Family bodyweight strength (15-20 min)
- **Saturday AM**: Training check-in conversation

## Age-Appropriate Guidelines

### 12-Year-Old
- 3-4 short runs/week (15-25 min, easy pace)
- Bodyweight strength 2-3x/week (15-20 min)
- Minimum 2-3 rest days/week

### 10-Year-Old
- 2-3 short runs/week (10-20 min, very easy)
- Bodyweight strength 1-2x/week (10-15 min, play-based)
- Minimum 3-4 rest days/week

### Dad (46, Marathon Training)
- 5-6 runs/week (building to 50-70 miles)
- Strength 2-3x/week
- Marathon-specific training

## Key Principles

- Never force participation
- Fun over performance
- Model, don't preach
- Listen more than talk
- Adjust as needed
- Safety first (watch for injuries)
